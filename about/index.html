About Me

I have 11 years’ experience working as a software developer with a broad knowledge base of skills. I started programming on my own at the age of 12, back then my first languages were Basic and 4 years later C++. That was my hobby. That’s how I got into the software world and since then I have experience of working with a wide range of technologies and languages, distributed and cross-functional teams.

I’ve been constantly trying to keep up and grow, love my profession and feel very lucky when thinking that on the other hand software development is my hobby. I like solving algorithmic and math problems as well as researching more elegant solutions. On the other hand I like innovations and open source projects.

Currently I occupy my time by working as a principle software engineer on large enterprise projects. Occasionally I speak at scientific conferences, write articles and work on innovative software projects.
